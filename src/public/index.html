About Us


The world is filled with conflicts and tension. One of the main conflicts that people struggle with is, mental health. Because of diverse cultures, mental health is treated and perceived differently around the world. With the inconsistent knowledge on mental health, a person would have trouble managing their own mental struggles and moods.


Sometimes people are more comfortable when they understand their situation and feel in control of their lives. Here we introduce WorryLess. A journaling app in which users can record their daily mood levels and worries. They are able to set up a “worry time”, a time-boxed session in which they will reflect on their worries and personal conflicts. We are building this app to help people deal with their worries and internal conflicts, specifically catering to people who struggle with anxiety and their emotions.
